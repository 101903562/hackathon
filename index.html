Problem Statements

By the various societies of Thapar

ROTARACT CLUB

We wish to develop a platform for people looking for blood in emergency conditions in Patiala and tricity region. A web or app based platform where people seeking blood can put up their requirements which includes blood types and place where it is required and the people whose data base is fed into the system are notified of the same and also people can volunteer to donate blood if they want to.

LitSoc

(1) For LitSoc yearly magazine Avant Garde create a platform where interested students can submit and editors can review the entries.

(2) Create a Web Platform where LitSoc's subsociety can conduct online quizzes. Include the following functionalities: - Timer for every question - Dynamic Leaderboard (updates after every round) - Ensure participant cannot open another tab during the quiz.

PRATIGYA

The objective of this problem is to connect a village(as many people as possible) to the head of the village(sarpanch).

-The website/app must be very simple from a user point of view.

-Some common problems of a village like, waste management, irrigation issues, crop burning, toilets, drainage problems etc, should be conveyed in on touch.

-For rest of the problems, there should be a timeline where to post(in local language).

-The website/app also should be connected to the nearest police station.

-Announcements made by sarpanch should be notified to the villagers.

-Website/app must contain a panic button which alarms sarpanch, police station, neighbours, and few selected people(selected by the user himself).

-The app/website has to be in Hindi, English, local language(Punjabi).

ECON

An app for live trading where teams can trade among themselves , they will need a login ID and password , they can trade among themselves but the caps are they cannot sell 30% +- the current market price. It should keep record of every transaction from which to which team.

Har Hath Kalam

(1) CHILD MONITORING SYSTEM

Har Hath Kalam India Association requires a web portal to track the performance of all the underprivileged kids who were sent to school by the organization, which will be further integrated with our website www.harhathkalam.org. This portal will be a medium of communication between the school faculty and HHK to monitor the attendance and academic performance of the kid. The portal will have two types of users. The school faculty or authorized HHK volunteer, any one of them will upload the initial details (name, class, address, roll no. and parents’ names etc.) of the kid and the school staff will update the attendance and marks of each quarterly exam regularly. This portal will be used by more than a hundred schools and details of thousands of kids will be entered in the nearby future so the database of the portal must be designed and optimized accordingly. The viewer must be able to view the data section wise, class wise, school wise also.

(2) BEGGAR REPORTING APP

Har Hath Kalam has an Android App which needs to be updated urgently. This is beggar reporting app where anyone can report a beggar by uploading a picture of a beggar on the App and the necessary details which are further accessible by the HHK representative who takes care of the follow up of the case. However, a lot of features need to be added. The Admin accessible features must be added according to needs. The App must be able to send a notification to the case reporter or all the donors after a successful follow up of a case. An authentic pledge counter must be included where people can pledge to not give alms to anyone. An interactive GUI must be created simultaneously. It must include an easy to use the feature where HHK representatives can upload news and events regularly on the App. The link to App is : Click here

entrepreneurship development cell

Design a login portal for an event (say event 1) and give rights to admin for further proceedings so that he or she can proceed further, After this, design a dashboard for registrations purpose and a button to upload documents on google drive followed buy sub events.

For user : Take name,password,email id,phone number, roll number,branch,year and provide a link to admin in the dashboard for further event registrations.

ENACTUS

Our organisation Enactus Thapar goals upon the use of innovation and business principles to improve the world. Create a website for us which must include:

I. Minimally designed, yet visually apealing Homepage containing:

  • Highlights of the events conducted by us in the form of text description/Pictures/Videos.
  • Little information about what Enactus is and what it does.
  • Short description of the projects done.

    • II. Separate pages giving detailed description about:

  • Goals and motives of Enactus Thapar.
  • All the projects conducted till date.
  • Events organised by us.
  • Our Contact Information.

    • III. A portal for members to login with the sections containing:

  • Discussions and conclusions outlined in meetings.
  • Personal details and contribution of the member that has logged in.
  • Tasks assigned to the member for upcoming events, etc.

    • IV. A portal for participants to register for events where the participant is able to find out everything about the event.

    V. There should be an integration of our Facebook, Twitter and Instagram handle to display images and posts into the website. Refer to sites.google.com/view/enactus-thapar
